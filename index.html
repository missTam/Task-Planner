      Take a moment to reflect

      Self-reflection is a very powerful technique which helps us become even better organized. In order to practice self-reflection, try to answer the following questions at the end of each day: How did you manage your daily tasks today? What went well? What helped you achieve your goals? How will you apply this again in the future? Where did you struggle? What obstacles did you encounter? What made it hard to finish particular tasks? What can you learn from that? By answering these simple questions, you are enhancing your self-management even further. Take control.
